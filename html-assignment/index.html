About Me

I am Mo Zhang from China. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Finance from WSU, United States, in 2017. After graduation, I worked at two different financial companies in China. Through these experiences, I realized my interest lies in self-employment. As a result, I decided to pursue a career as a YouTuber/Vlogger, which is why I chose to study the IMD.

After the 2nd weeks of learning about design courses, I realized that there are many career options beyond being a YouTuber, such as graphic designer, photographer, and web developer. This has allowed me to explore new fields I had never considered before while keeping my curiosity alive. As an American writer said,
Curiosity is the wick in the candle of learning.-William Arthur Ward
I believe that after graduation, I will discover even more career paths and opportunities.
